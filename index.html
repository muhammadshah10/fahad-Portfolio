About Me

Hello, I'm Muhammad Shah Fahad, a passionate and results-driven Mobile Apps Developer with over 4 years of experience in building robust, user-friendly, and high-performance applications for both Android and iOS platforms.

I specialize in creating seamless mobile experiences with clean, maintainable code and strong UI/UX focus. My work spans the entire app lifecycle — from concept to deployment — with a commitment to quality and scalability.

Here’s what I bring to the table:

  • Native Android Development: Expert in Kotlin and Java, crafting responsive layouts using XML, and managing efficient local storage via Room and SQLite.
  • Cross-Platform Apps with Flutter: Skilled in Dart to deliver fast, visually engaging apps for both Android and iOS from a single codebase.
  • Backend & API Integration: Experienced in integrating secure and scalable REST APIs, and working with databases like MySQL, PostgreSQL, and Firebase Realtime Database.
  • Development Tools & Agile Workflow: Proficient in Android Studio, VSCode, and Xcode. I follow Agile methodologies to ensure fast iterations, effective communication, and on-time delivery.

I’m always exploring new technologies and frameworks to deliver modern, scalable solutions that make a real impact.
